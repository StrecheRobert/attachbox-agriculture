About BEIA

BEIA is a Romanian technology company with extensive experience in the development and integration of smart systems based on IoT, communications, sensors, cloud platforms and data visualization tools. Through its research and innovation activities, BEIA supports the digital transformation of multiple sectors, including agriculture, environmental monitoring, water management, smart cities and cultural heritage.

Smart Agriculture Focus

In the agricultural sector, BEIA develops and integrates solutions that help farmers, researchers and stakeholders collect real-time data from the field. These solutions can include environmental sensors, soil monitoring stations, weather parameters, connectivity technologies and platforms for data visualization and alerting.

The objective is to support more efficient resource management, early detection of risks, improved crop monitoring and better decision-making based on accurate and timely information.
